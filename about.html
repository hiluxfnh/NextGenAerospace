About NextGen Aerospace Technologies

NextGen Aerospace Technologies was formed in 2025 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, by Eglah and a small team of educators, engineers, and storytellers. We are in our pilot phase, building simple but powerful learning experiences that introduce Tanzanian children and youth to the possibilities of aerospace.

Founded 2025 Dar es Salaam Led by Eglah & team Pilot programs in motion

Mission

Lower the barrier to aerospace literacy for Tanzanian learners through hands-on projects, playful media, and community mentors.

  • Prototype maker kits using locally available materials.
  • Blend offline-first lessons with simple digital tools.
  • Invite families and teachers into every design sprint.

What We're Building

Our immediate focus is to launch the first Nyota Junior learning lab, document everything we learn, and translate that into playbooks for other Tanzanian and East African classrooms.

  • Weekend discovery camps for ages 7-14.
  • Facilitator toolkits co-created with local teachers.
  • Pathways that point curious teens to mentors and internships.

Guiding Values

Honesty Care Curiosity Community

We build at a pace we can sustain, share real updates, and keep learners' safety and joy at the center.
